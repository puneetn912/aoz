Sustainable packaging & delivery - DJFresh utilises packaging and delivery methods that are sustainable and not harmful to the environment.

On the packaging front, we are using the good old, steel tiffin boxes. Besides contributing to a cleaner, greener and plastic-free environment, we also want to give our patrons the warmth and feel of eating straight out a tiffin box as if it is prepared in their own home. No matter where you are, ghar ka khaana will follow. We request our patrons to treat the dabba with utmost care and love.

In our endeavour to give back to the planet, we are going a step further by delivering our food in a more sustainable fashion. Contrary to the popular bike taxis, DJFresh satisfies hunger pangs of their customers by delivering food in a vehicle that runs on biodiesel.
